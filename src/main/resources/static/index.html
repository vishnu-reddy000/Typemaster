Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about standard typing evaluations on TypeMaster.

WPM stands for Words Per Minute. It is calculated by dividing total typed characters (letters, spaces, punctuation) by 5, and then dividing that by the duration of the test in minutes. This ensures short and long words are normalized into standard metrics.

Accuracy is the percentage of correctly typed characters relative to the total characters typed. If you type 100 characters and make 2 errors, your accuracy is calculated as 98%. TypeMaster counts backspaced corrections as mistakes to encourage typing precision.
