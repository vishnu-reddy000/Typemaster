Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2026. Your privacy and data security are central to our product engineering values.

1. Information We Collect

TypeMaster collects minimal information required to deliver an optimized skill-building experience. The information we process includes:

  • Account Information: If you choose to register, we collect a unique username, your email address, and a hashed password (secured on our servers using the BCrypt algorithm).
  • Performance Logs: We save details of completed typing tests (such as Words Per Minute (WPM), accuracy percentages, character keys pressed, and timestamps) to generate statistics and achievements.
  • Local Data: For guest sessions, this history is written directly to your browser's local storage and is not saved to our databases.

2. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use standard cookie technologies to remember your theme choices and active sessions. These cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information.

3. Google AdSense Advertising Disclosure

We partner with third-party networks, specifically Google AdSense, to serve advertisements on TypeMaster. Google uses cookies, including the DART cookie, to serve customized ads to users based on their visits to this site and other websites on the internet.

Users may manage these tracking preferences or opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security Standards

All registered credentials and statistics are stored securely in our database. We employ modern cryptographic protocols (including password salting and BCrypt hashing) to protect against unauthorized data exposure.

Please note that while we take strict measures to safeguard your information, no digital network or transmission is entirely secure. We encourage users to maintain unique passwords for their TypeMaster accounts.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

We believe in complete transparency. You have the right to request deletion of your account and related records. To submit a deletion request, please reach out to our team at vishnumatamala@gmail.com, and we will purge your statistics from our database within 48 hours.
